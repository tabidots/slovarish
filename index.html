Slovarish has evolved

As of 7 July 2025, the previous version of Slovarish has been retired. It has moved here, so you should update your bookmarks if you wish to continue using it. However, please note that the app was abandoned long ago (ClojureScript apps are just too difficult to maintain!)

Meanwhile, you are encouraged to check out the latest evolution of the dictionary at slovarish.com. The name is the same, though it is a fundamentally different app.

Thank you for your interest in Slovarish!
